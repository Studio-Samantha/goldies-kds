Policy, Privacy & Ownership

DrinkFlow KDS is an artist-built, analyst-minded kitchen display and reporting tool by Studio Samantha. Goldie's KDS is the current customer implementation for Goldie's Coffee & Goods.

Data Use

Since this app tracks order flow, drink counts, timing, and owner-only revenue reports, I want to make sure you’re comfortable with how the data is used. Each shop's operational data is for that shop's internal dashboard unless permission is given otherwise. If a customer is later used as a case study, I will only use approved screenshots, anonymized stats, or quotes that are reviewed first.

The app uses operational data from connected services to show order status, daily counts, drink statistics, timing, and owner-only drink revenue reports. Financial reporting is intended for authorized owners only.

Founding Customer

Goldie's Coffee & Goods is the first live customer implementation and has approved being referenced as a founding customer and case study. Goldie's receives lifetime free use of its current DrinkFlow KDS implementation as a thank-you for helping prove the product in a real opening-week environment. Public screenshots, customer quotes, or specific business statistics should still be approved before use.

Email Updates

The Learn More page may collect email addresses from people who want updates about DrinkFlow, future features, onboarding, or availability. Those emails are stored for Studio Samantha follow-up and are not part of a shop's internal KDS order data.

Access & Security

  • The main KDS requires a staff password before order data is shown.
  • Owner revenue reports require a separate owner password.
  • Owner report access is intended only for approved owners or managers.
  • Support access or troubleshooting should be limited to what is needed to keep the app working.

Customization & Themes

Branding, colors, background images, watermark styles, category labels, report wording, and future visual themes can be customized by shop. Example screenshots or mockups may show a specific customer setup, but category names and branding are not universal across every business.

Support & Updates

Setup can include launch support, branding, Square® connection, custom domain setup, and post-launch help for basic upkeep. Larger feature requests, workflow changes, new reports, expanded customization, or source-code handoff terms require a separate written agreement or quote.

Proprietary Code

The application code, custom implementation, design system, reporting logic, deployment setup, and related technical materials are proprietary property of Studio Samantha unless a separate written agreement says otherwise. A customer's business data, shop information, and approved brand materials remain that customer's property.

Third-Party Services

This app can connect to third-party platforms including Square®, Supabase®, Render, and Vercel. Those services may have their own terms, privacy policies, logs, and security controls.
