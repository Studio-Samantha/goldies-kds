Privacy & Ownership

Goldie's KDS is a custom kitchen display and reporting tool built by Studio Samantha for Goldie's Coffee & Goods.

Data Use

Since this app tracks order flow, drink counts, timing, and owner-only revenue reports, I want to make sure you’re comfortable with how the data is used. The data is for Goldie’s internal dashboard unless you give me permission otherwise. If we later use Goldie’s as a case study, I’ll only use approved screenshots, anonymized stats, or quotes that you review first.

The app uses operational data from connected services to show order status, daily counts, drink statistics, timing, and owner-only drink revenue reports. Financial reporting is intended for authorized Goldie's owners only.

Access & Security

  • The main KDS requires a staff password before order data is shown.
  • Owner revenue reports require a separate owner password.
  • Owner report access is intended for Blake, Claire, and any person they approve.
  • Support access or troubleshooting should be limited to what is needed to keep the app working.
