What makes it different

A lightweight KDS tuned for the way drink shops actually work.

Most kitchen display systems are built around entrees, courses, kitchen stations, and large restaurant workflows. DrinkFlow KDS focuses on the counter: readable tickets, fast status moves, counted drink categories, and owner visibility.
