Buyer questions

Clear answers before setup.

Best fit

Coffee shops, smoothie bars, tea shops, bakeries with drink volume, and counters already using Square® for orders or payments.

The goal is not to replace Square®. It is to give drink shops a cleaner KDS, cleaner drink analytics, and reports owners can actually use.

What does DrinkFlow have that Square® KDS does not focus on?

DrinkFlow focuses on configurable drink analytics, average drinks per order, 2+ drink order rate, daily shop guidance, branded PDF reports, and custom setup.

Does it replace Square®?

No. DrinkFlow connects through the Square® API, so the shop should already use Square® for orders or payments.

Does DrinkFlow work with POS systems besides Square®?

Square® is the first live connector. Shopify, Clover, Lightspeed, and other POS workflows can be scoped by request when a shop's order data is accessible through an API or webhook.

What screens work best?

The best setup is often a TV or monitor as the shared real-time board, with staff moving tickets from an iPad, phone, mouse, remote, or nearby control device.

Can customers see order numbers too?

Yes. A customer-facing orders-up display can be added a la carte for coffee shops, food trucks, and pickup counters that want customers to see when orders are ready.

What happens to retail items?

Retail items like merch, packaged goods, gifts, and add-on retail can stay out of drink totals so owner reports focus on actual drink behavior.
