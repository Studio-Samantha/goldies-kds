The goal is not to replace Square®. It is to give drink shops a cleaner KDS, cleaner drink analytics, and reports owners can actually use.

What does DrinkFlow have that Square® KDS does not focus on?

DrinkFlow focuses on configurable drink analytics, average drinks per order, 2+ drink order rate, daily shop guidance, branded PDF reports, and custom setup.

Does it replace Square®?

No. DrinkFlow connects through the Square® API, so the shop should already use Square® for orders or payments.

Can it run on a phone?

Yes. It can run on an iPad, simple phone screen, or handoff display, depending on the shop's counter setup.

What happens to retail items?

Retail items like merch, packaged goods, gifts, and add-on retail can stay out of drink totals so owner reports focus on actual drink behavior.
