What it can do

Live Square® drink orders in a simple board, customer names and employee tracking when available, owner reports, hourly volume, history, and daily order lookup.

Training mode is included for practice without live data, and each version can be branded for the coffee shop or drink counter using it.

The private owner portal can track drink revenue, hourly volume, saved advice snapshots, and month-by-month CSV downloads.
